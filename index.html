Degas Card Matching Game

To play, click a box to pull back the curtain and reveal a card.
Repeat to reveal a second card.
Now that you have two cards revealed, click a third to see if it matches one of the first two.
If you have a match, they will remain "flipped".
If your cards do not, they will show the back of the card, and you must try again.
Repeat until you have found all the matches.
Have fun, or as they say in Paris, S'amuser!
